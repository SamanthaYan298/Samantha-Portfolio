Bow of Infinity
Movie Poster

This poster was designed using Adobe Photoshop for a holographic game-themed movie. The protagonist, a fire-element archer, stands out against a predominantly dark background. It enhances the contrast between the character and the vector elements.

Neon blue holographic interfaces and project emergency warnings strengthen the suspenseful atmosphere. The design seamlessly blends fantasy and sci-fi elements, immersing the viewer in an action-packed world.

Ming Dynasty Eternal Radiance
Museum e-Advertisings

This promotional poster is designed for the National Canadian Museum of History. The Ming Dynasty phoenix crown is the centerpiece in this exhibition.

The design includes one for the museum's large outdoor screen and a smaller version for iPad displays. The poster incorporates traditional Chinese elements, featuring imperial yellow and auspicious cloud motifs. The central ink symbolizes the passage of history, adding a sense of mystery and timelessness to the design.

Spirit of The Mountain
Fantasy Book Cover

This cover is designed for the fantasy novel Spirits of the Mountain. It includes the book cover design, the publisher's logo, and vector elements.

Lionhorn is my original character, a fusion of four animals: a gypsy horse, deer antlers, resplendent Quetzal, and lion. This book cover emphasizes nature and the ethereal creature, capturing a sense of mysticism.

Green Sprout Brunch
Restaurant Menu

Green Sprout Brunch is a family-friendly restaurant that embraces organic and healthy eating as its core features. Catering primarily to young, health-conscious individuals and their children, the restaurant offers a welcoming environment where families can enjoy nutritious meals together.

The brand's green and yellow color palette reflects its commitment to nature and vitality. Adorable vector elements add a playful and inviting touch, capturing the attention of both young adults and children.

Chinese Style Illustrations
Postcard & Greeting Card

This postcard and greeting card are designed for lovers of New Chinese-style illustration.

“Lotus Dreamscape” is a fantasy-inspired postcard that blends traditional Chinese aesthetics with a mystical atmosphere. This limited-edition postcard was released during Lichun (the Beginning of Spring), celebrating the tradition of spring outings.

The second card is a 2024 Dragon Year greeting card, created for families celebrating the Lunar New Year, symbolizing prosperity, unity, and tradition.
