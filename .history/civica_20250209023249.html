Civica

Civica is an AI-powered legal app designed to help underprivileged women navigate complex legal challenges. It integrates an AI chatbot, document simplifier, and step-by-step guidance while connecting users to trusted resources like professionals and support groups.

Design Process

The survey shows that most legal issues affect immigrants unfamiliar with laws and rights.

The primary user is a single mother lacking financial support, and struggling to find help, while the secondary user is a high school student who fears confrontation and lack of legal support.

Userflow

After initial brainstorming and research, Civica identified three main app features: a chatbot, a document simplifier, and free support resources.

My team interviewed a lawyer and analyzed seven representative cases. We decided to include three cases: Employment Inequity, Driving Test Dispute, and Restraint Orders to develop a step-by-step guidance template.

Design

Colors

This color palette is carefully selected for its harmonious balance of cool and warm tones, creating a visually appealing aesthetic.

Light blue and pale purple create a serene atmosphere, while light yellow adds warmth and approachability. Using purple as the main interface color brings elegance and a calm feeling. Those colors can ensure a strong contrast with black text.
