Everlasting Vintage

Everlasting Vintage
Advertisement Posters

This project is designed for a vintage clothing store, including a set of three advertisement posters along with different logo designs. It conveys a message that emphasizes classic, timeless concepts.

"Fashion in Endless Rebirth, Classics that Never Fade."

Timeline – 2 Weeks

Software – Adobe Illustrator

Design Rationale

Everlasting Vintage offers services centered around the renewal, recycling, and remaking of vintage clothing, reflecting its commitment to sustainable fashion. Their style is based on vintage aesthetics from the 1920s to the 1990s, emphasizing timeless designs. The main color is deep purple, which expresses mystery, symbolizing luxury and timeless elegance.

This brand targets eco-conscious fashion lovers, collectors, and individuals seeking unique, sustainable pieces. Through the reuse and transformation of old garments, Everlasting Vintage aims to reduce environmental impact and extend the life cycle of clothing. All items are unique and handmade.
