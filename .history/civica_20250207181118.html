Hello, I am Clotho

Clotho is a personal wardrobe assistant app designed to solve common wardrobe challenges. Research indicates that Millennials are the ideal target audience. With established careers, they have greater spending power and a higher tendency for impulse purchases. Many Millennials struggle with choosing outfits or remembering what clothes they own, often leading to unused items. The app’s UI and UX were developed by a team of three using Figma and React.

Colors

This color palette is carefully selected for its harmonious balance of cool and warm tones, creating a visually appealing aesthetic.

Light blue and pale purple create a serene atmosphere, while light yellow adds warmth and approachability. Using purple as the main interface color brings elegance and a calm feeling. Those colors can ensure a strong contrast with black text.

Typography

Open Sans is a modern, clean sans-serif font that enhances readability, especially in small sizes. Using it as body text.

Roboto Slab is a serif font that adds a sense of structure and formality, making it great for headings and key content.
