Titania

This is a website designed for a local jewelry brand, focused on premium handcrafted and customized jewelry. It includes pages for Home, Collection, Product, and Shopping Cart.

Design Rationale

Titania is an online platform for a jewelry brand inspired by the fairy queen from “A Midsummer Night’s Dream”. It offers a range of mid to high-priced, simple, and elegant products crafted from gold, silver, pearls, and titanium. The brand's mood is friendly, warm, peaceful, and blessed, with designs that are imaginative, nifty, dreamy, whimsical, and fashionable.

The target audience is females of all ages, especially in their late 20s and early 30s. They are looking for unique gifts for loved ones, commemorating significant occasions, or surprising a partner with something special. Titania appeals to those seeking a novel and one-of-a-kind jewelry experience.

Logo Designs

The logo design is a combination of three elements: Fairy wings, the letter “T” from our brand name “Titania”, and the fairy queen Titania's crown. The bottom wing is filled in as it emphasizes the sparkles. This gives the wings logo element a whimsical and pixie like feel. The letter “T” also represents a butterfly's body and antenna.

Branding

Colors

The coral feminine tones were chosen for their delicate, fairy-like qualities and cottage core aesthetic, which resonate with the target audience.

The monochromatic palette of red and pink symbolizes femininity, love, youth, and playfulness, creating a sophisticated yet energetic feel.
