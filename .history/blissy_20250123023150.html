Tropical Sparkling Water

Blissy offers organic, healthy sparkling water with tropical flavors like mango, pineapple, and coconut. Each flavor is unique in its illustration and vibrant color.

Concept Design
Package

“Experience the vibrant, sun-soaked flavor of the tropics with every sip. “

Timeline - 2 Weeks (2024)

Software - Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Design Rationale

Blissy sparkling waters are designed for the Canadian market, targeting individuals seeking a clean, healthy alternative to traditional water.

Compared with pure water and soft drinks, sparkling water is an excellent choice for its organic and fresh qualities, as it is free of calories, sugar, and artificial additives. Its effervescent bubbles provide a refreshing taste and are available in a variety of flavors.

Design Concept

Tropical fruits are often associated with exoticism, health, and vacation vibes, aligning perfectly with the image of sparkling water as a better-for-you beverage.

Pineapple and coconut sparkling waters are widely accepted by Canadians. To stand out in a crowded market, I decided to introduce a unique flavor like passion honey.Since the company focuses on tropical-flavored sparkling water, I chose a flamingo as the mascot.
