Nested
In Nature
Surreal Structures

A magazine about modern fantasy architecture, including four buildings designed by MAD Architects.

"Embracing nature within the heart of the city."

Timeline – 1 Months

Software – Adobe Indesign

Design Rationale

Urban architecture often feels monotonous. As people walk the crowded city streets day after day, this magazine is designed for weary office workers and architects, offering a fresh perspective on urban design. The decision to include these four modern buildings is based on their philosophy of embracing nature within the city.

It captures the ever-changing essence of architecture through the lens of the four seasons and the contrast between day and night. It highlights designs that ease the boundaries between nature and the urban, embracing smooth design that mimics natural landscapes. With a strong focus on fostering connection, the team aims to encourage interaction and engagement with the environment.
