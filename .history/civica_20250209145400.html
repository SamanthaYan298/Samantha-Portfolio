Civica

Civica is an AI-powered legal app designed to help underprivileged women navigate complex legal challenges. It integrates an AI chatbot, document simplifier, and step-by-step guidance while connecting users to trusted resources like professionals and support groups.

AI Chatbot

Provides Instant Help

Chat with the chatbot using voice or text commands for AI-powered legal advice, ensuring accessibility for everyone.

  • Get Legal Advice in Multiple Languages

Document Simplifier

Simplifies Legal Jargon

Simplifies complex legal documents, making legal documents more accessible to users who might not be familiar with legal language.

  • Get Document Interpretation with OpenAI
  • Save Scanned Files and History

Support Group

Offers Comprehensive Legal Help

Access a wealth of legal resources, from professional advice to step-by-step guidance, ensuring users have the support needed to succeed.

  • Access to Legal Document Templates
  • Referral to Trusted Legal Professionals

Design Process

The survey shows that most legal issues affect immigrants unfamiliar with laws and rights.

The primary user is a single mother lacking financial support, and struggling to find help, while the secondary user is a high school student who fears confrontation and lack of legal support.

Userflow

After initial brainstorming and research, Civica identified three main app features: a chatbot, a document simplifier, and free support resources.

My team interviewed a lawyer and analyzed seven representative cases. To develop a step-by-step guidance template, we selected the following three cases:

  • Employment Inequity
  • Driving Test Dispute
  • Restraint Orders
